4.2.2 Vertical velocity

 

Comment: The vertical velocities are very reasonable, comparable to those achieved at world-class level [6]. However, one aspect which could be crucial is that peak velocity of the jump is reached prior to take-off. Thus, velocity at take-off is 5-10% lower than the peak, which is a significant decrease. This could be due to either timing or lack of effort following through. This is something we will look for in the rest of the analysis.
